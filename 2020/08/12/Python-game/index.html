GAME INTRODUCTION

I’ve been teaching myself Python for about two months, and I’ve learned that there’s a classic game in Python called Alien Invasion. In Alien Invasion, players control a space ship that initially appears at the bottom and center of the screen and can be moved from side to side using the keyboard, as well as attacking using the space bar. The game starts with a group of aliens appearing in the upper half of the window and moving down, and the player shoots them. After the player has eliminated all the aliens, there will be another group of aliens, and their movement speed will be faster. Whenever an alien crashes into a player’s space ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the player loses one space ship, and when the player loses three space ships, the game is over.
