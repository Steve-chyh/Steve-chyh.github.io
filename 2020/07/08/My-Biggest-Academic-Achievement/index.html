My Biggest Academic Achievement

As a Chinese high school student, studying takes up a large proportion of my life. When it comes to academic achievements, I believe that I have a lot, but the biggest I think is the senior school entrance examination.

In China, the senior school entrance exam is valued as much as the college entrance exam, because it determines what kind of high school you go to, and thus determines your future development. Therefore, every junior high school student in China needs to spend a long time to prepare, and I am no exception. Since the senior high school examination will involve all the knowledge of junior high school for three years, and even the knowledge of primary school, it is very time-consuming to review for the senior high school examination.

When we entered the third year of junior high school, I have arranged my goal of taking the best high school exams in Nanjing. To reach the target, I made a study plan that is in line with my ability which made me exhausted every day for the next semester. I ever want to give up in the last few weeks, because my schedule is too strict on myself, but I remembered how much effort I had put in, so I just gritted my teeth and kept going.

Compared to the time of revision, the exam seems to be over in the blink of an eye. However, when the results came out three weeks later, I was really disappointed due to low marks which didnâ€™t meet my expectation. Although there were lots of problems with my final result, this is my greatest achievement, because the exam was the largest one that I have ever sat. In addition, my grade was among the top 10 percent among the 52,000 examinees in Nanjing.

Overall, I do believe that was a beginning of my further study. I will continue to improve and meet their expectations.
