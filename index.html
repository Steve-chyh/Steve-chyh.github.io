Summary of the antecedent

In my last article, I successfully used Function check_events() and Function update_screen() to simplifiy the program. In this blog, I’d like to make the aircraft to move.

Response buttons

Every time we press a button, we register an event in PyGame. Events are all fetched through the method pygame.event.get(), so in the function check_events(), we need to specify which types of events to check.Each keystroke is registered as a KEYDOWN event. When we detect a KEYBDOWN event, we check to see if the key that was pressed is specific.For example, if we press the right arrow key, we increase the rect.centerx value of the ship to move the ship to the right.

Allow constant movement

When we hold down the right arrow, we want the ship to keep moving to the right until the player releases it.We will have the game detect the Pygame.keyup event so that we know when the player releases the right arrow;We will then combine the KEYDOWN and KEYUP events with a flag called moving_right to achieve the continuous movement.(moving_right is a Boolean variable that is set to True when the right arrow key is held down)

GAME INTRODUCTION

I’ve been teaching myself Python for about two months, and I’ve learned that there’s a classic game in Python called Alien Invasion. In Alien Invasion, players control a space ship that initially appears at the bottom and center of the screen and can be moved from side to side using the keyboard, as well as attacking using the space bar. The game starts with a group of aliens appearing in the upper half of the window and moving down, and the player shoots them. After the player has eliminated all the aliens, there will be another group of aliens, and their movement speed will be faster. Whenever an alien crashes into a player’s space ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the player loses one space ship, and when the player loses three space ships, the game is over.

My Biggest Academic Achievement

As a Chinese high school student, studying takes up a large proportion of my life. When it comes to academic achievements, I believe that I have a lot, but the biggest I think is the senior school entrance examination.

In China, the senior school entrance exam is valued as much as the college entrance exam, because it determines what kind of high school you go to, and thus determines your future development. Therefore, every junior high school student in China needs to spend a long time to prepare, and I am no exception. Since the senior high school examination will involve all the knowledge of junior high school for three years, and even the knowledge of primary school, it is very time-consuming to review for the senior high school examination.

When we entered the third year of junior high school, I have arranged my goal of taking the best high school exams in Nanjing. To reach the target, I made a study plan that is in line with my ability which made me exhausted every day for the next semester. I ever want to give up in the last few weeks, because my schedule is too strict on myself, but I remembered how much effort I had put in, so I just gritted my teeth and kept going.

Compared to the time of revision, the exam seems to be over in the blink of an eye. However, when the results came out three weeks later, I was really disappointed due to low marks which didn’t meet my expectation. Although there were lots of problems with my final result, this is my greatest achievement, because the exam was the largest one that I have ever sat. In addition, my grade was among the top 10 percent among the 52,000 examinees in Nanjing.

Overall, I do believe that was a beginning of my further study. I will continue to improve and meet their expectations.
